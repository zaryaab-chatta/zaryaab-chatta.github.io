University

I am currently attending University at Toronto Metropolitan University. I am now in my fifth year here having started in Fall 2020. I am expecting to graduate this coming June 2025.

Some of the courses I have taken to date include:

    1. Learned about modeling business processes using Business Process Modelling Notation (BPMN)
  2. ITM 820 - Information Systems Security and Privacy
    1. Learned about industry best practices relating to computer security
  3. ITM 200 - Fundamentals of Programming
    1. Covered the fundamentals of programming going over arrays, loops, validation, etc
  4. ITM 301 - IT Infrastructure
    1. Introduction to computer hardware/software and communication networks
