Search Engine Optimization (SEO) Techniques

On-page SEO involves optimizing the content and structure of a webpage to make it more visible to search engines. This includes adding relevant keywords, improving meta descriptions, and enhancing site usability. These optimizations help search engines rank pages higher and provide users with valuable content (SEO Resellers Canada, 2024a).

Source: SEO Resellers Canada. (2024). *On-page SEO strategies*. Retrieved from seoresellerscanada.ca.

Local SEO is designed to help businesses appear in location-based searches. This includes creating a Google Business Profile, optimizing Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP) information, and gaining local reviews. Effective local SEO strategies improve visibility for users searching for services nearby (SEO Resellers Canada, 2024b).

Source: SEO Resellers Canada. (2024). *Local SEO techniques*. Retrieved from seoresellerscanada.ca.

Link building involves creating high-quality backlinks from reputable websites to improve your website's credibility and authority. Google evaluates these links to determine a site's trustworthiness, helping it rank higher in search results. Link building is a crucial part of off-page SEO strategies (SEO Resellers Canada, 2024c).

Source: SEO Resellers Canada. (2024). *Importance of link building*. Retrieved from seoresellerscanada.ca.

SEO Techniques Used

Three primary SEO techniques were implemented in the making of this webpage:

  • Meta Description: A meta description was added in the `` section of the HTML. This provides a concise summary of the webpage's purpose to search engines, improving the likelihood of clicks in search results.
  • Keywords: Relevant keywords, such as "SEO," "SMO," and "web design," were included in the `` tag to help search engines identify the topic of the webpage.
  • Heading Tags: Structural heading tags ("h1", "h2") were used throughout the document to organize content hierarchically. This makes the page more readable for users and improves its SEO ranking.
