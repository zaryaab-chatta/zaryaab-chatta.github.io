High School

After graduating from Teston, I attended high school at Maple High. I spent four year’s here from 2016-2020. The school slogan was:

"Working Together Toward Student Success"
Some of the courses I took include:

  1. Financial Accounting Principles
    1. Developed basic accounting knowledge
  2. Intro to Computer Science
    1. Learned entry level Python and Eclipse
  3. Economics
    1. Learned about Micro&Macro Economics
  4. Data Management
    1. Learned about statistics in modeling as well as methods for organizing data
  5. Canadian and International Law
    1. Developed a basic understanding about the Canadian and international legal system
Course Description
Financial Accounting Principles Developed basic accounting knowledge
Intro to Computer Science Learned entry level Python and Eclipse
Economics Learned about Micro&Macro Economics

Out of all the courses mentioned, the most notable to me was Intro to Computer Science. This is because I was introduced to computer programming which I have found useful in my professional career. With the entry level skills I acquired I was able to improve my resume and use the knowledge learned in university courses such as this one!

Educational Awards: I was awarded the honour roll each year of high school. This was as a result of me achieving above 80% each semester.
